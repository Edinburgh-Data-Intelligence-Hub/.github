## üéØ **About us**

</div>
<br>
<div>
The Data Intelligence Hub (DIH) is a two-year Research Training and Support unit within the Biomedical AI CDT at the University of Edinburgh, focused on developing industry-ready researchers and enhancing the quality of academic outputs. We support PhD students and research teams by embedding modern engineering practices, providing targeted technical mentorship, and strengthening links between research and industry.
